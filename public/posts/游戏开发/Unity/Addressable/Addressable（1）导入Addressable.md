# 一、 导入Addressable

## 知识点一：导入Addressable

- 打开packageManager

  - 选择Unity Registry

  - 找到加载的Addressables，点击安装

    ![image-20231017205342289](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/liujianjie/Image/main/ImgFloder/202311200015168.png)

  - 安装成功可以看到示例工程

    ![image-20231017205434345](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/liujianjie/Image/main/ImgFloder/202311200014464.png)

  - 检测是否安装成功

    ![image-20231017205456169](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/liujianjie/Image/main/ImgFloder/202311200014476.png)

## 创建配置相关文件

### 方法一：打开资源组窗口

- 方法

  - 选择Asset Management - Addressables - Groups

  - 点击create Addressables settings

    ![image-20231017205722094](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/liujianjie/Image/main/ImgFloder/202311200014484.png)

- 结果

  ![image-20231017205738016](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/liujianjie/Image/main/ImgFloder/202311200014488.png)

### 方法二：在Inspector窗口中为资源勾选Addressable

- 方法

  - 选择Scene

  - 勾选Addressable
